Legend has it a man known only as Peter created a quiz. Inboxes across the island of Ireland eagerly awaited it's link every Friday. It became a symbol of hope for the underpaid and despressed. However neither quiz nor Peter have been seen in over 2 years.

After a lot of training montages I have decided to take up the mantel of Peter. Using the powers of Javascript I will craft a quiz out of the workshop of life. Behold the Friday Quiz and may the questions forever be in your favour.
